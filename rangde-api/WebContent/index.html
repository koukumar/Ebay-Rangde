Why Rang De Life?

Healthcare. Or the lack of it.
20% of Urban India & 28% of Rural India* is unable to pay for life and livelihood-threatening medical treatments. Rang De supports low income households with low cost credit to work hard and rise, but we realized that it took just one medical emergency to push them back to the brink.

* Source: 2004 Annual Health Survey by NSSO.
